Support

JIGGO is a private, on-device coach for grooming, style, physique, confidence, and discipline — without the toxic playbook.

No analytics. No accounts. No ratings. No comparisons. Insight, not judgement.

Need help?

JIGGO is built local-first — there are no accounts to recover and no servers involved. For questions, bug reports, or feedback, email meloxkgz@icloud.com and you'll get a reply directly.

The AI Coach

The Coach is optional. It uses your own Anthropic API key, stored only in your device's keychain — your conversations go straight from your device to Anthropic and nowhere else. If you never add a key, nothing leaves your phone.

Your data

Everything you create stays on your device and can be exported at any time from Settings. See the Privacy Policy for the full detail.
