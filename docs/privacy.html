JIGGO MAXXING

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 May 2026

JIGGO MAXXING is built local-first. The short version: your data lives on your device, we run no servers, we have no accounts, and we collect no analytics. This page is the long version.

No analytics. No accounts. No ratings. No comparisons.

What is stored, and where

Everything you create in JIGGO MAXXING — scans, journal entries, plan progress, wardrobe items and saved outfits, daily-nudge history, coach conversations — is stored only in the app's local storage on your device. None of it is transmitted to us. We do not operate a backend that could receive it.

Photos

When you take or pick a photo for a scan, it is processed entirely on your device and saved into the app's private storage area. Photos are never uploaded. The scan's dimension readings are generated locally.

The AI Coach

The Coach is optional. To use it you provide your own Anthropic API key, which is stored in your device's secure keychain and is never sent to us. When you send a message to the Coach, that message — together with a small amount of app context you can see — is sent directly from your device to Anthropic's API to generate a reply. It is sent nowhere else. Anthropic's handling of that request is governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy. If you never add a key and never open the Coach, nothing leaves your device at all.

Notifications

The optional daily reminder is scheduled locally on your device. There is no push server. We never receive a device token.

What we collect

Nothing. There is no analytics SDK, no advertising identifier, no crash reporter, no account system, and no tracking of any kind. We have no way to know who you are or that you use the app.

Data you export

The app lets you export your data to your clipboard and re-import it. That action is entirely under your control and happens only on your device.

Children

JIGGO MAXXING is intended for adults. It is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated date above will change. Because we hold no contact information, material changes will be communicated in-app.
